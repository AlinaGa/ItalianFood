Traditional Italian Food

Welcome to our authentic Italian restaurant!
Experience the true taste of Italy in a cozy and warm atmosphere that will make you feel right at home.

  • Bolognese

    Spaghetti bolognese consists of spaghetti with meat sauce and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese. Spaghetti bolognese is one of the most popular pasta dishes eaten outside of Italy.

  • Carbonara

    Carbonara is a Roman pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork and black pepper. The dish took its modern form and name in the middle of the 20th century.

  • Napoli

    Pasta Napoli is a classic italian dish of spaghetti served with a basic tomato-based sauce. Outside Italy, the basic sauce is vegetarian. We offer it with or without meat.
